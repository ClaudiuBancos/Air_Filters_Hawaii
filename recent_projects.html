Recent Projects

The Kau Hospital on the Big Island hired Air Filters Hawaii to evaluate their facility and we installed IQAir Chemisorber filters in their operating rooms, waiting room, cafeteria and office areas.

Air Filters Hawaii has begun work on the Cone residence in Kihei which will be the first whole house vog free home utilizing whole house HVAC systems with Perfect 16 Multigas filters and HealthPro Plus filters.

The Reipurth residence in Hilo was gutted for moister and mold problems associated with its oceanfront location. It is being renovated to protect the home from oceanfront moisture using dehumidification and air purification. Air Filters Hawaii provided the interior air quality consultation for the project and provided air filters for the entire residence.

We are designing vog safe rooms for three schools in the Hilo area.

We recently completed a very successful installation for the Kulani Correctional Center. Twenty five IQAir VOG filters were installed across 8 buildings and 25,000 sq ft of space. The State of Hawaii Department of Health (Hazard Evaluation Division) carefully monitored our work. It was a great success! Everyone is breathing better now.

The National Park Service located at the Volcanoes National Park purchased a GC Chemisorber for their offices.

The Hilo King Kamehameha School has requested proposals from Air Filters Hawaii for custom made high volume vog filters for two 7,000 sq ft cafeterias with 20 ft ceilings to be used as vog safe rooms for 1,700 students, faculty and staff. The proposed designs will create 12,000 cubic feet per minute per room of purified air totaling five air changes per hour.

The Moore Residence in Olowalu, Maui is being renovated to remove moisture problems associated with its oceanfront location. Air Filters Hawaii is optimizing the indoor air quality in this home by using Perfect 16 whole house in-duct filters, in-duct dehumidifiers and GC Multigas filters for vog.

Air Filters Hawaii designed a vog safe Library for the Keaau High School, on the Big Island. The design has been accepted by the school and the school is awaiting funding to purchase installation of the filters.

The Geraci Residence in Plantation Estates, Kapalua, Maui is a new 10,000 sq ft estate under construction with many LEED points including photovoltaics, solar hot water heating, 10,000 gallons of rain water catchment, super insulation, pervious hardscape, gravel driveways, natural ventilation, low albedo materials, 50,000 sq ft of natural habitat and undesturbed landscape, low voltage lighting, low VOC paints.

The Lether Residence on Molokai is a new 6,300 sq ft ocean front residence in final design with many LEED points including photovoltaics, solar hot water heating, 4,000 gallons of rain water catchment, pervious hardscape, gravel driveways, all natural ventilation, low albedo materials, four acres of natural habitat and undesturbed landscape, low voltage lighting, low VOC paints.
